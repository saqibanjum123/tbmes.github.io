    Welcome

    A note from the Principal and Director during the COVID-19 Pandemic:

    It is now four months of virtual classes for us all. Over 6000 students and 300 teaching and IT staff have had a new experience of e-teaching and learning. I will fail in my duty if I do not recognize the presence of most of you parents who have been so helpful in the smooth conduct of these classes sitting by the side of the young learners and also giving your inputs to the teachers. This new cooperation between parent, student and teacher has been a pillar of strength for all in this new pattern of learning. Frankly I am surprised by the enthusiasm and grit shown by one and all in adapting this modern approach.

    I am sure that our students will find this mode of education very useful in future anywhere in the world.

    Our teachers and IT staff have been putting in their best efforts in getting across to the students virtually which has been possible at such a large scale only because the TBMS schools had been well prepared in the development of a very good IT infrastructure over the last many decades.

    I congratulate all of you for having completed the academic requirements of the students of the four schools up to the end of Term-I. We have also been concerned with the physical absence of students from school and therefore we tried to introduce physical education and other interactive activities for all students. Teachers were involved with students in morning assemblies, physical exercising, general knowledge, moral/value education, art and craft and so on.

    A new feature started online by the Principal and Director is called “KNOW YOUR ALMA MATER/ SCHOOL” in which we are keeping students and parents informed of the heritage of TBMS schools, digging deep into our history since 1876, so that the present students (and even the new staff) may not be deprived of the true culture and activities that have made our schools the most sought after in the valley. I have noticed by the response of many parents who are former pupils that this daily blog is much appreciated.

    I assure all the stakeholders that the TBMS society will always continue to give the people of the valley our best in educating your child in body, mind and soul.

    With prayers for your good health

    Parwez Samuel Kaul
    Principal and Director
    Tyndale-Biscoe & Mallinson Society, Srinagar
    July, 2020
